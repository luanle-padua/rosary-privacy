# Privacy Policy for Praying the Rosary

**Last Updated: November 30, 2025**

## Introduction

Praying the Rosary ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Praying the Rosary does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Photographs or media files

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Your prayer preferences (selected mystery, date)
- Audio playback settings (volume, speed, language)
- Visual preferences (weather effects, text size)
- App settings (auto-advance, ambient sounds)
- Dynamic Island preferences (if enabled)

**Important:** This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## In-App Purchases

The app offers optional donations through In-App Purchases:
- Espresso ($3)
- Americano ($5)
- Cappuccino ($10)
- Affogato ($25)

These transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for information about how Apple handles purchase data.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
The app uses Apple's native frameworks:
- **ActivityKit** - For Dynamic Island Live Activities (iOS 16.2+)
- **StoreKit** - For In-App Purchases
- **AVFoundation** - For audio playback

No data is shared with third parties beyond Apple's standard iOS frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- No data transmission over the internet
- No server-side storage
- No account creation required
- Data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Delete Data:** Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- **Reset Settings:** Use the app's settings to reset preferences
- **No Account Deletion Needed:** No account is created, so there's nothing to delete

## Audio Content

### Prayer Recordings

The app includes professionally produced audio recordings of Catholic prayers in seven languages:

**Voice Technology:**
- **English, Spanish, French, Italian, Korean, Portuguese:** Generated using ElevenLabs AI voice synthesis technology
- **Vietnamese:** Recorded by the app developer using their own voice

**Prayer Content:**
All prayers are traditional Catholic prayers that are in the public domain. We make no copyright claims on the prayer texts themselves.

**Technical Details:**
- All audio files are bundled with the app
- Stored locally on your device only
- Not transmitted over the internet
- Available completely offline
- Audio recordings are protected by copyright and may not be extracted, reproduced, or redistributed without permission

**Attribution:**
